Session Prep

PREPARE YOUR
STRATEGY

⚠  THIS IS NOT A PITCH — This is a strategy session. Come ready to teach, not to sell.

Your session should answer these 7 questions:

1

What is the strategy?

Name it. Define it in one sentence.

2

Why does it work right now?

What market conditions, platform changes, or timing makes this effective today?

3

What results have you gotten?

Specific numbers. Revenue, ROAS, conversion rates, growth metrics.

4

Who is it best suited for?

What type of business, niche, or stage benefits most?

5

How does someone implement it?

Step-by-step. Tools, templates, sequence, timeline.

6

What are the common mistakes?

Where do people go wrong? What should they avoid?

7

What's the first action step?

One thing they can do today to start seeing results.

Start Here

Run these five skills first.

These are the Amplifier Skills. They produce reference files about you, your business, your voice, and your buyer. Every other skill in the system reads those files at runtime. Skip them and Claude writes for "a generic expert." Run them and Claude writes for you.

The 5 Amplifier Skills

Complete them in order. Each one builds on the last.

1
Personal Brand DNA Extractor

Point it at your existing material — courses, emails, transcripts — and it builds eight reference files covering your voice, credentials, client transformations, origin story, philosophy, and avatar. Every writing tool in the system reads these at runtime.

Run this first. From that moment on, every skill sounds like you instead of like ChatGPT.

2
Unique Genius Discovery

Interviews you, reviews your work, and finds the specific intersection of strengths, experience, and perspective that makes you hard to replace. That answer feeds into at least seven other skills.

Running it early compounds — it shapes your positioning, content, offers, and sales conversations.

3
Client Compass

Maps your ideal client's actual psychology: their beliefs, struggles, exact language, and the thoughts blocking them from buying. Produces a one-page Client Compass Card you keep open during sales calls.

After this runs, every marketing skill stops guessing and starts targeting real human beings.

4
Copywriter Setup

A one-time interview (20-30 minutes) that captures your identity as a writer, your niche, voice preferences, and methodology. Produces four files that every copywriting skill loads automatically.

You stop re-explaining yourself every time you ask Claude to write something.

5
Psychographic Excavation

Goes deeper than a standard avatar doc. Produces three to five forensic buyer profiles covering the primary wound, core belief, competing impulses, decision-making pattern, and the exact language your buyer uses.

Copy written from this depth of understanding converts differently than copy written from a demographics worksheet.
